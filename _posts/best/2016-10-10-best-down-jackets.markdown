In this blog post I have selected the [best down jackets](#the-best-down-jackets-of-2017) of 2017 which I would consider to buy if I were to get a  new one for my next backpacking or winter adventure.  

A down jacket is one of the most essential piece of clothes when planning an overnight trip outdoors or during winter time. A down jacket keeps you much warmer compared to a fleece jacket.

Usually, I wear a [fleece jacket](http://www.hikeventures.com/best-fleece-jackets/) as a mid-layer when I go hiking. When it get’s windy I pull on a [wind jacket](http://www.hikeventures.com/best-windjackets/).  If I sit around in my camp I switch my fleece to a down jacket.   

Further, I rather carry a [synthetic insulated jacket](http://www.hikeventures.com/best-synthetic-insulated-jackets/) with me than a down jacket if I am planning to go hiking in damp or humid conditions as down may not insulate very well once it get’s wet.   

Also have a look on our [FAQ](#faq-on-down-jackets) to learn more about down jackets.

### When to wear?  
Down jackets are best worn when it’s not that humid. I have down jackets for different seasons. A thin down jacket for chilly 3-season use when I go backpacking and another thicker down jacket when I go for example on a winter trip to Lapland.  

---  

### What is fill power?  
Fill power indicates the fluffiness of down. The more fluffy the down the less down I need compared to less fluffy down. Jackets with high fill power are usually more expensive than down jackets with less fill power.  

---  

### How to wash?  
I wash my down jacket by hand. However, you can also gently wash your down jacket in a washing machine.   

---  

### How to fluff?  
A big tumbler can be used to dry the down jacket. Also add a few tennis balls to get the down fluffy.  

---

### How to pack and store it?  
I have one dedicated dry bag where I stuff my down jacket and my sleeping bag in. Down products need much less space than synthetic products and the best way to get as much space as possible is to compress down in a compression bag.
