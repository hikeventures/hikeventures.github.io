The Mountain Equipment AR Ultralite 2 is an inexpensive tent which seems to be suitable for trips in moderate conditions. Here is a preview of the Mountain Equipment AR Ultralite.

## Features
The Mountain Equipment AR Ultralight 2 weights around 1700 grams, comes with 2 entrances, a vestibule, glowing zippers to easily find your tent during the night and can also be supported by trekking poles.

The guy ropes of the Mountain Equipment AR Ultralite 2 needs to be pulled very tight. The inner tent seems to hang a bit lose and may cause your sleeping bag of getting wet.
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/90204224@N07/13637480103/" ><img src="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5097/13637480103_59e6cab27c_b.jpg" width="1024" height="683" ></a>
There are 2 meshes on each site supporting air ventilation.
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/90204224@N07/13637496083/" ><img src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2921/13637496083_b672cdfeef_b.jpg" width="1024" height="683" ></a>
With two entrances it is easy to get in/out of the tent.
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/90204224@N07/13637551795/" ><img src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3688/13637551795_d5f9051d7d_b.jpg" width="1024" height="683" ></a>
The pegs and poles of the Mountain Equipment AR Ultralite 2 are made of Aluminium.
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/90204224@N07/13637524555/" ><img src="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5315/13637524555_745a8c097d_b.jpg" width="1024" height="683" ></a>
