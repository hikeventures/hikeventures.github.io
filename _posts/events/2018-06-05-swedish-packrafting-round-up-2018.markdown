Last weekend we visited again the Voxnan river in Sweden to participate in the Swedish Packrafting Round-Up 2018. It was good to see that there were plenty of new faces from different countries and also 3 female paddlers. In general there was a lot of paddling but most of us probably enjoyed the Vinstr√∂men rapid. The weather was perfect as we had only sun and blue sky and just a one hour period of rain.
