luminAID is a solar-powered inflatable light which can be used to light up your camp or you can attach it to your packraft if you want to paddle at night.<br><br>
<img src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7381/13970539645_6d777e495b_c.jpg">
<!--more-->

##Features
The LuminAid weights 85 grams and can offer light for 16 hours after a charging it for 7 hours. There is a low and a high light setting. The LuminAid is also inflatable, water-resistand and can float.<br><br>
